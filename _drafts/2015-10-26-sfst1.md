The Harvey Mansfield transducer.

    % SFST tutorial, 1


    % step 1:
    % B:A | C:B | D:C | F:D



    % SFST 2:
    % run fst-print and notice 2 generators for D
    %[B-F]:[A-D]

    % SFST 3:
    % [B-D]:[A-C] | F:D


    % SFST 4:
    %$mansfield$ = [B-D]:[A-C] | F:D
    %$mansfield$


    % SFST 5:
    ALPHABET= [B-D]:[A-C]  F:D
